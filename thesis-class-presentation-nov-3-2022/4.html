Research through Design is “an approach to conducting scholarly research that employs the methods, practices, and processes of design practice with the intention of generating new knowledge...[such as]...novel perspectives that advance understanding of problmenatic situations; insights and implications with respect to how specific theory can best be operationalized in a thing and artifacts that both sensitize the community and broaden the space for design action” [Zimmerman & Forlizzi].
